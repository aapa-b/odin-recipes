Arrosto d'Agnello con Erbe e Aglio

Description

This recipe comes from the south of Italy, where lamb is simply roasted, with garlic and wild mountain herbs.

Ingredients

  • 1,5kg of lamb leg
  • 2-4 spoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, cut in half
  • 2 branches of fresh sage
  • 2 little branches of dried rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 branches of fresh thyme
  • 3/4 cups dry white wine
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • fresh herbs for garnish

Steps

  1. Remove all fat excess. Spread with oil. Make small cuts in the skin and put garlic in some and herbs on the others.
  2. Put the rest of the herbs above the lamb and leave it in a fresh place for at least 2 hours before cooking it. Preheat the oven at 190ÂºC.
  3. Put the lamb on a big oven plate around herbs. Spread two tablespoons of olive oil above it, season it with salt and pepper. Roast it for 35 minutes, spreading its own juice above it.
  4. Pour the wine above the lamb. Roast it 15 minutes or until it's done. Put it on a tray that can resist the heat, tilt it and remove the fat with a spoon, you can use it for making a sauce. Cut the meat and serve it separated from the juices. Garnish with fresh herbs.
